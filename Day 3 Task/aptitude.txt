Question 1
If the ratio of A:B is 3:5 and B:C is 2:3, find A:C.
Solution:
     A:B=3:5,
     B:c=2:3
     A:B=3*2:5*2=6:10
     B:c=2*5:3*5=10:15
     A:B:C=6:10:15
Answer A:C=10:15
