BLOCK 3 – COMMUNICATION PRACTICE

Speak for 3 minutes on:

Explain one challenge you faced while learning coding

Include:
- What was the challenge?
- Why was it difficult?
- How did you overcome it?
- What did you learn from it?

When I was in my first year, I didn’t know what coding or programming really meant. 
It was completely new to me, and I had no basic understanding of how it works.

The challenge I faced was starting from zero in my second year. It was difficult because 
coding involves logic, syntax, and problem-solving skills. At first, I couldn’t understand errors, 
and even small programs seemed confusing.

To overcome this, I started learning step by step. I practiced basic programs, watched tutorials, 
and asked my friends and teachers for help. Slowly, I began to understand concepts and gained confidence.

From this experience, I learned that consistency and practice are very important in coding. Even if 
something feels difficult at the beginning, we can improve with time and effort.
