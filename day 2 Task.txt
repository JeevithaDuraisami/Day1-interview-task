BLOCK 1 – APTITUDE (Profit & Loss)

Question 1
A shopkeeper buys a book for ₹400 and sells it for ₹480.
Find the profit percentage.
  solution:
    cost price=400
    selling price=480
    profit=selling price-cost price
          =480-400
    profit=80
    profit percentage=(profit * 100)/cost price
                        
                     =(80 * 100)/400
                     =20%
  Answer: profit percentage=20%
                       
Question 2
A man buys a watch for ₹1500 and sells it for ₹1200.
Find the loss percentage.

solution:
     cp=1500
     sp=1200
     loss=cp-sp
     loss=1500-1200=300
     loss %=(loss * 100)/cp 
            =(300 * 100)/1500
            =20%
  Answer: loss percenatge=20%
